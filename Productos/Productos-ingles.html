3D Benches

Urban bench manufactured using 3D printing with recycled plastics, designed for use in public spaces such as streets, parks, school playgrounds, and rest areas. Its design is customizable, weather-resistant, and low maintenance, contributing to the circular economy and improving urban well-being. It is available in individual or modular versions, allowing adaptation to different environments and needs.

3D Trash Bin

Urban trash bin manufactured with recycled materials and integrated with an IoT device that includes an ultrasonic sensor to measure fill levels. This system makes it possible to optimize waste management, reduce collection costs, and improve urban cleanliness through remote monitoring of the bin's status, enabling more efficient and sustainable management of public spaces.

Product Preview in VR

Virtual reality service applied to urban furniture, allowing the visualization and planning of product installation in real environments before manufacturing or placement. It is aimed at municipalities and urban furniture companies and includes specific training for using the tool, reducing errors, improving decision-making, and optimizing the final results of each project.
