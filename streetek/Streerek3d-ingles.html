Streetek 3D

We are a technology company committed to sustainability and urban innovation.

Our vision as a company

At StreetTek 3D, we want to change the way urban spaces are designed and built. We strive for more sustainable, inclusive, and participatory cities, where urban furniture is not an imposed element, but an integrated part of the environment and the people who use it. We combine technology, design, and sustainability to offer customized, efficient, and environmentally friendly solutions, using recycled materials and responsible manufacturing processes.



Our products

We design and manufacture urban furniture using large-scale 3D printing, recycled materials, and recyclable filament. Our catalog includes benches and trash bins adapted to each space and need. In addition, we offer the possibility to preview products in virtual and augmented reality environments before manufacturing, allowing clients and citizens to participate in the final design and ensuring perfect integration into the urban environment.
